Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Returns & the Snacker Guarantee

We stand behind every bag. If your snacks arrive damaged, or you're simply not happy, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll refund or replace your order — no need to ship food back.

  1. Email hello@craversbest.com with your order number.
  2. Tell us what went wrong (a photo helps for damaged items).
  3. We'll issue a refund or send a replacement, your choice.

Wrong or missing items

If something's missing from your box or you received the wrong flavor, let us know within 7 days and we'll ship the correct item right away.
