Shipping & delivery

How much is shipping?
Flat-rate shipping is $4.95, and it's free on every order over $35.

How fast will my order arrive?
Orders ship within 1–2 business days and typically arrive in 3–5 business days within the continental US.

Do you ship internationally?
Not yet — we currently ship within the United States. Join the CraversClub and we'll let you know when we expand.

Products & ingredients

Are your snacks vegan / gluten-free?
Many are! Look for the Vegan and Gluten-Free tags on each product page, or shop those collections directly.

How fresh are the snacks?
We make in small batches and rotate stock constantly, so your snacks ship fresh. Each bag has a best-by date printed on the back.

How should I store them?
Keep bags sealed in a cool, dry spot. Once opened, clip them shut and enjoy within a week for best crunch.

Bundles

How does "Build Your Own Bundle" work?
Pick any four full-size bags and we'll pack them together at a bundle price. Mix sweet, salty, and spicy however you like.

Orders & returns

Can I change or cancel my order?
Email us within 2 hours of ordering and we'll do our best to catch it before it ships.

What's your return policy?
Love it or your money back. If something's wrong, contact us within 30 days for a refund or replacement. See Shipping & Returns for details.
